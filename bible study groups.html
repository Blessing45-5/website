Bible Study Groups

We want to help you build your people in knowing and walking daily with our Heavenly Father. The most effective way to do that is through time with Him in His word.

For this, you don’t need a preacher. You don’t need a theological book. You don’t even need to be able to read. Across thousands of years, God our Father through the Holy Spirit has superintended His words to be written by tens of authors.....for people like you.

We have a free resource for you to simply download and use with your people. It will lead them in encountering God regularly in His word. Over 26 books of the Bible are provided and will help your people over several years develop their own time with God on their own.

Additionally, you can use it as a resource for the house groups and discipleship groups you are starting. To grow your people in their faith, and in telling their grace story to those around them.

The material is available in the following Indian languages. If it not in yours, talk with us and we will help it be translated. Additionally, if your people cannot read, we will do everything we can to get to you preloaded Bibles in your language.

All you need to do is tap on the following link and someone will begin the process of getting you access.
